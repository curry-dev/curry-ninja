ABOUT THE ARTIST

Hello! Curry Ninja here.
I make anime fanart and original art! Currently I am a uni student studying engineering in computer science. And so I draw as a hobby! Completely self taught and mostly inspired by anime; I've been drawing since the last 13 years. I can draw portraits, aesthetic interior doodles, fanart, original characters, and much more! I started making digital art in 2020. I have a dream of becoming an animator some day!
